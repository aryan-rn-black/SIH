NEP 2020

The executive summary highlights how manual scheduling in Indian Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) leads to significant inefficiencies, wasted resources, and a heavy burden on educators and students, hindering progress towards the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020's goals. It argues for adopting a "Smart Classroom & Timetable Scheduler" as a strategic necessity, not just an upgrade, to unlock institutional potential, improve outcomes, and support the NEP's student-centric, flexible vision. This approach is presented as essential for modernizing the system and addressing the deep link between operational inefficiencies and high-level educational policy.
